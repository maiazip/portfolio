About me

Hello human beings, I'm Matheus Maia Bastos, a 23 years old brazilian developer with an ongoing degree in systems analysis and development. My history in programming began 4 years ago when I started to dive into the development world, with a lot of dedication and conviction. I am a person who dreams big and likes minimalist and modern designs I continue to seek to grow more and more in the area to gain the space that I believe I deserve Currently I like to develop projects with React, Tailwind and Typescript, using the best libraries we have on the market. I hope you want me to be part of your team, I guarantee you won't regret it.
